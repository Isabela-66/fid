Couscous Salad with Peas and Mint

This is barely a "recipe" since I just threw it together and didn't pay much attention to measurements. But it turned out to be tasty, so I thought I'd share.

Ingredients

  • 1 package couscous
  • 1 can chicken broth (or veggie broth if vegetarian)
  • 1 to 1/2 cups fresh peas, shelled
  • 3-4 scallions, sliced thin
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (or to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook couscous according to package directions, using chicken broth instead of water. Let cool.
  2. Boil peas for about 3 minutes until bright green and tender. Rinse in cold water and cool.
  3. Slice scallions, chop mint, toast pine nuts, and prep any extra veggies.
  4. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make dressing.
  5. Toss couscous with peas, mint, scallions, pine nuts, and dressing. Serve right away or chill.
