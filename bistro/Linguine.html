Linguine with Clam Sauce

I don't know how authentic this recipe is, but it’s quick and delicious. You can make it lactose-free by substituting dairy-free margarine. This recipe serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chopped clams (tuna-sized cans)
  • 1/2 head of garlic, chopped fine
  • 1 shallot, chopped fine
  • 2-3 tbsp butter (or margarine)
  • 2/3-3/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 lb. linguine

Instructions

  1. Boil water for linguine and salt it well.
  2. Open clams and pour liquid into a measuring cup, saving about 1 1/2 cups liquid. Add water to make 2 cups total.
  3. Sauté shallot in butter until translucent. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  4. Add clam juice and wine. Simmer until reduced by half.
  5. Add clams and cooked pasta. Toss together and cook until pasta is done and clams are warmed through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Add extra butter if desired.
