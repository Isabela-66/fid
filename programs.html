Program Overview

Our programs are grouped by age and experience so that each player trains with others at a similar level. Within every group, players are pushed individually and given clear feedback on what to focus on between sessions.

Foundation Skills — Ages 8–11

Perfect for younger players who are still building coordination and comfort on the ball.

  • Ball mastery moves and fun 1v1 challenges.
  • Simple passing patterns and combination play.
  • Small-sided games to learn spacing and teamwork.
  • Confidence-building environment where effort is praised.

Development Squad — Ages 12–14

Designed for players who already have basic skills and want to add speed, strength, and smarter decision-making to their game.

  • Position-specific drills for defenders, midfielders, and forwards.
  • Training based on real game moments: pressing, counter-attacking, switching play.
  • Increased physical demands with agility and strength work.
  • Introduction to basic video analysis and self-evaluation.

Performance Group — Ages 15–18

This group is for serious players committed to training like they are in a professional setting.

  • High-intensity exercises with limited rest, similar to academy sessions.
  • Focus on tactical understanding and game management.
  • Individual targets set for each training block.
  • Feedback designed to prepare players for trials and higher levels.

Goalkeeper Union

Our goalkeeper-specific pathway runs alongside all age groups.

  • Shot-stopping, handling, distribution, and 1v1 scenarios.
  • Leadership training: communication with defenders and organising shape.
  • Footwork and fitness tailored to the demands of the position.
