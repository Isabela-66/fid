Weekly Training Timetable

Our fictional timetable below shows a typical training week at the academy. Each session includes a warm-up, main theme, and small-sided game section.

Day Time Group Focus
Monday 17:00 – 18:30 Foundation Skills Ball mastery, dribbling, and 1v1 attacking
Wednesday 18:00 – 19:30 Development Squad Pressing, counter-attacking, and quick combination play
Friday 18:30 – 20:00 Performance Group Game-like scenarios, finishing, and tactical shape
Saturday 09:30 – 11:00 Goalkeeper Union Handling, footwork, and 1v1 situations

Summer Camp in England

Every July, we imagine running an intense one-week summer camp that offers:

  • Two training sessions per day.
  • Video analysis evenings with professional match clips.
  • Team-building activities and classroom workshops.
  • Friendly matches at the end of the week to showcase progress.

For real inspiration and to explore how top English clubs organise themselves, you can visit the official Premier League club list here: Premier League Clubs — Official Site .

What Players Should Bring

  • Proper football boots and shin guards.
  • Water bottle and light rain jacket (this is England after all!).
  • Notebook for writing down feedback and personal goals.

Being prepared shows professionalism and respect for the game, and it helps players get the most out of every session.
