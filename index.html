Welcome to the Academy

Elite England Soccer Academy is a high-performance fictional academy located near Liverpool. Our mission is to create a professional-feeling environment where young players train with purpose, passion, and top-level standards.

Every session is built around ball mastery, decision-making, intensity, and the modern style of English football seen in the Premier League.

Why Train With Us?

  • Elite-level coaching tailored to each player’s strengths.
  • Modern training patterns used by Liverpool and top academies.
  • Focused sessions in a positive, confidence-building environment.
  • Smaller groups for more touches and more attention.
